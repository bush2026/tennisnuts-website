The Story

From an Idea to a Championship in the Making

The inaugural edition was a statement. Under the aegis of PMDTA, organised jointly by Tennisnuts, Capovitez, and India Professional Engineering — with Manegrow as title sponsor — 24 teams from across Pune and PCMC gathered at the MSLTA courts in Mhalunge Balewadi between 7–10 October 2022.

The inauguration was graced by Shri Ankush Shinde Saheb, Police Commissioner of PCMC, alongside Abhishek Tamhane and Mihir Divekar. Sudhir spoke about Sopal Sir's immense contribution to tennis and his devotion to the Mhalunge facility — a facility he looked after like his own. Abhishek Sir moved the audience with memories of his childhood and how Sopal Sir's support helped him win for Team Maharashtra.

What followed was three days of relentless competition — 46 ties, almost entirely self-refereed in the true spirit of the game. Nail-biting matches, surprising upsets, and moments of individual brilliance showcased the depth of tennis in Pune. Two teams even travelled from Phaltan to compete — a testament to the championship's reach from day one.

The closing ceremony was blessed by Shri Suhas Patil Sir (Deputy Sports Director, Govt. of Maharashtra), Shri Janath Tekale Sir (Rtd. Deputy Sports Director), and Shri Jayant Sopal Sir. Tennisnuts' YouTube livestreams were watched 500+ times — on short notice.

46 Ties in 3 Days

24 teams, 46 ties, zero major delays — teams played the entire tournament in the spirit of the game without needing referee intervention in almost all matches. Pune tennis showed its depth and discipline.

🏡
Phaltan Came to Play

Two teams from Phaltan made the journey to Balewadi and competed beautifully — among several clubs new to the tournament scene who made their mark at the very first edition.
