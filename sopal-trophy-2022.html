Highlights

Moments from the Inaugural Edition

🎖️
Inaugurated by Police Commissioner

The championship was inaugurated by Mr. Ankush Shinde (IPS), Police Commissioner of PCMC — a mark of the event's standing in Pune's sporting calendar from day one.

🏆
192 Players, 24 Teams

The inaugural edition drew an outstanding response — 192 players from 24 club teams across Pune and PCMC confirmed their entries, reflecting just how much Pune's tennis community was ready for this championship.

🤝
A Community United

Tennisnuts Panchayat 1 & 2, FC, Maharashtra Mandal, Golden Boys, Aces United, Forest Hunters and many more clubs competed side by side — rivals on court, friends off it.
