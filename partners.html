Why Partner With Us

Part of Pune's Tennis Story

Tennisnuts is supported by a community of partners who share our love for tennis and commitment to growing the game in Pune. We've built one of the city's most engaged tennis communities — 500+ active players, regular events, and a growing digital presence.

Our partners get genuine visibility at events, authentic social media features, and a direct connection to Pune's most active tennis audience. We don't do generic sponsorships — we do real partnerships.

Logo placement at all Tennisnuts events, tournaments, and clinics throughout the year

Featured posts and stories across our Instagram, WhatsApp, and newsletter channels

Direct access to 500+ active tennis players across all skill levels and demographics

Potential features on the Tennis Bytes podcast and Tennisnuts content platforms
