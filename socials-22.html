The Story

Edition Recap

Happy Makar Sankranti — and short notice, as ever. The response was great because the love for the game was real. Socials #22 ran a back-to-back singles and doubles format for the first time — exhausting by design, a test of fitness more than anything else. Nobody fainted on court. Someone did get a cramp after going home. That counts as a win.

The final was the match of the day. Deepak came into it running on fumes and an energy drink, then proceeded to produce a marathon chase of Joy — who operates at a level the rest of the field looks up to. 'Pro-Amateur' doesn't quite cover it. Joy simply sets a different benchmark. That Deepak pushed him to the end says everything about both players.

"Deepak fought in the finals after some energy drink — like a marathon catchup with Joy. Super played."

Special moments scattered through the day: Jitendra Sir and Aditya arriving as a duo (the Bryan Brothers of Tennisnuts), Mane Sir's approach forehand followed by a roar after the winner, Abhinav the Coach playing alongside a 60+ player from his morning batch — who was visibly thrilled to have their coach competing alongside them. Chirag Sir coming from across the city just to play. All of it is what Socials is for.

Thanks to Kumar for taking the time to get trophies and medals sorted. To Prashant for on-court support throughout the day. Looking forward to seeing Sameer Sir, the PCMC gang, Sunil Lulla and partners at the next one.
