The Story

Tournament Recap

The PMDTA Tennisnuts Tour kicked off its 2025–26 season with the S35+ Open Doubles — and true to form, it refused to make things easy. Rain delays tested everyone's patience, the draw was confirmed with less than 16 hours' notice, and three powerful groups promised no easy matches from round one.

"Ajinkya and Sumant had never played together before — but on the day, they played like they'd been partners for years."

The real story of the tournament was the winning pair. Ajinkya and Sumant took the court as first-time partners, no shared practice, no rehearsed patterns. What they had instead was instinct, trust, and the kind of on-court communication that can't be manufactured. They read each other effortlessly, covered ground without words, and when the final arrived, they were simply the better team — composed, clinical, and completely in sync.

Sunil and Joy made them earn every point. The runners-up were gutsy and relentless, bringing intensity to every rally and pushing the final to its limits. Their campaign through the draw was impressive, and they left the court knowing they'd given everything. A podium fully deserved.

Beyond the finalists, this was a tournament full of quality. Three powerful groups meant that upsets lurked around every corner, and the matches across all groups were competitive, well-contested, and exactly what the PMDTA Tennisnuts Tour was built for — serious tennis in a genuine community setting.

A big thank you to Sportsjam for their continued support in making these events possible. Sincere thanks to Venky, Atul, and Amit K for all the effort behind the scenes, and to Mahesh Sir for keeping the courts in shape despite a stubborn weather forecast.
