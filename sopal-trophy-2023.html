Highlights

What Made 2023 Special

🏟️
New Venue — NCL Pashan

The championship moved to NCL Tennis Complex, Pashan — a superb facility that provided the perfect backdrop for an intensely competitive day of inter-club tennis.

⚔️
A Final to Remember

PYC A and Tennisnuts Rogers went head-to-head in a tightly contested final. PYC A ultimately prevailed 17–13, with the 80+ doubles pair sealing the title in style.

🌱
Growing the Championship

With Tennisnuts taking the organisational reins under PMDTA, the second edition built on the foundation of 2022 and raised the bar for inter-club tennis in Pune.
