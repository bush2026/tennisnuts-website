The Story

Edition Recap

Announced on Thursday. By the following Sunday: 24 pairs confirmed and 6 NextGen junior players registered. Overwhelming response, as always. But this edition was different — Daksh, Neev, Rama, Ram, Karan, and Ishan joined the adults, playing doubles alongside amateurs who are three times their age, and made the courts feel alive with a different kind of energy.

The final was between Neev & Dr Ravi and Daksh & Amit — four players split between two generations. Neev and the Doctor won. For Neev, this was his first Socials winner's trophy. For Dr Ravi, it was another title to add to a collection that continues to grow. Daksh and Amit Kindo made the final together — a pairing of junior sharpness and experienced doubles reading that was genuinely competitive throughout.

"Neev's first winner trophy at Socials. And Dr Ravi making it two in a row. NextGen meets the Doctor."

Organising and not playing was a different kind of satisfaction — watching the kids bring energy to the courts with beautiful rallies and shots. Venky was behind the scenes making sure everything ran. Sportsjam provided vouchers generously. The NextGen players made Socials #30 genuinely special — and ensured everyone left thinking about what the future of this community looks like.

Thanks to Venky for all the running around in the background — trophies, logistics, everything. To Amit for his reliable support as always. To Sportsjam for the generous vouchers. To all 6 NextGen players — Daksh, Neev, Rama, Ram, Karan, and Ishan — for making this edition something special.
