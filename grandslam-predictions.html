About Grand Slam Predictions

Your tennis brain, on the line.

Four times a year, the world's best gather for a Grand Slam — and every Tennisnut gets to put their tennis brain to the test. Before each day's play begins, members fill in a fresh set of predictions for that day's matches via a Sportsjam form. Every correct call earns points on the community leaderboard.

It's not just about picking the favourite. Deep rounds reward bold calls. A wildcard semi-finalist no one saw coming? That's points in the bank. The player who watches closely, knows the draws, tracks form — they rise. The casual pick-the-world-number-one player? They get found out.

Each edition has its own podium. At the end of the season, the community's sharpest tennis mind earns the ultimate bragging rights: the Tennisnuts Slam Caller.

How It Works

Three steps to the podium.

🎯

Predict Each Matchday

Before each day's play begins, members fill in a Sportsjam form with their picks for that day's matches. A fresh form, every matchday, across the full fortnight of a Slam.

📊

Earn Points

Every correct pick earns points — deeper rounds score higher. Picking the winner is good. Correctly calling who they beat in the quarters is better.

🏆

Claim the Podium

Results are tallied after the final. The top three predictors take the edition podium. The highest scorer across all four Slams becomes the season's Slam Caller.
