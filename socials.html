What are Socials

Tennis meets community. Every time.

Tennisnuts Socials is a regular open social tennis event held at courts across Pune. Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or you've been playing for decades — if you love tennis, you belong here.

Each session is carefully organised to ensure fair matchups, partner rotation, and most importantly, a great time on court. Nobody sits out. Everyone plays. And the post-match chai? Basically mandatory.

Over the years, Socials has grown from a small group of friends into Pune's most loved tennis community event. It's where friendships form, weekend habits start, and tennis becomes genuinely addictive.
