The Program

12 Weeks to a Better Game

The Academy is our flagship coaching program. Over 12 weeks, you'll work one-on-one and in small groups with certified coaches to systematically improve your game from the ground up.

Every Academy batch is kept small — a maximum of 6 players — so every member gets meaningful court time, personalised feedback, and a training plan built for them. We use video analysis every 4 weeks to track and show your progress.

The Academy is designed for intermediate and advanced players who are serious about improvement and willing to put in the work. If that's you, we'd love to have you.

Curriculum

What You'll Learn

🎯
Technical Fundamentals

Grip, stance, and stroke mechanics — rebuilt from the ground up if needed. Film analysis sessions every 4 weeks give you objective evidence of how your technique is evolving.

♟️
Tactical Awareness

Reading the game, shot selection under pressure, and point construction against different playing styles. You'll learn to think like a tennis player, not just hit like one.

⚡
Physical Conditioning

Footwork drills, court movement patterns, split-step timing, and tennis-specific fitness. Being physically prepared is the difference between watching the ball and reaching it.

🧠
Mental Toughness

Pre-match routines, handling pressure points, staying present during tight matches. The mental game is half the battle — we take it seriously from day one.
