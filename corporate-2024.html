What Made the Inaugural Edition Special

🏢
12 Teams

The inaugural edition brought together 12 corporate teams from across Pune — companies big and small united by their love of tennis and a healthy competitive spirit.

🎾
Doubles Only

Pure doubles format meant every player depended on their partner. Teamwork wasn't just encouraged — it was essential to winning every single match.

☀️
Day-Long Event

From the first match in the morning to the trophy presentation in the evening, it was a full, action-packed day of non-stop doubles tennis across Pune.
