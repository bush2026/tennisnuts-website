Tennis for Good

Using the sport we love to make a difference in Pune's communities.

Our Mission

More Than a Game

We believe tennis can be more than a game. Tennisnuts runs free clinics for underprivileged youth, donates equipment to schools, and partners with local NGOs to bring the sport to corners of Pune where access is limited.

Founded on the belief that tennis is for everyone, our social work arm is driven by volunteers from within our own community — members who give back not because they have to, but because they want to see the sport they love grow beyond club walls.

"Every child deserves a chance to hold a racket."

Our Approach

We partner with schools, NGOs, and community organisations to identify children who would benefit most. Programmes are structured, consistent, and designed with long-term impact in mind — not just a one-day event.

Volunteer Driven

All our social programmes are run by Tennisnuts members who volunteer their time, skills, and equipment. Every racket donated and every hour coached comes from our community.

What We Do

Our Initiatives

🎾
Free Youth Clinics

Monthly free group sessions for children from low-income communities across Pune. We provide rackets, balls, and qualified coaching at no cost. Every child leaves having played a proper game of tennis.

🏫
School Tennis Program

Partnering with 3 Pune schools to introduce tennis as part of their physical education curriculum. We have donated full equipment sets and provide coaching support to help PE teachers deliver quality sessions.

🎁
Equipment Drive

Collecting used but playable rackets and balls from club players and redistributing them to new learners. One donated racket can change a child's introduction to the sport. Drop-offs accepted at all Tennisnuts venues.
