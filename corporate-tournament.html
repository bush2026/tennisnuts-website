About the Tournament

Where Offices Compete on the Court

The Tennisnuts Corporate Tournament brings offices and companies together through the shared language of tennis. Teams of colleagues enter as a unit, compete in a doubles-only format, and leave with stronger bonds and unforgettable memories.

It's a single-day, fast-paced tournament designed to be fun for players of all skill levels — from seasoned club players to complete beginners picking up a racket for the first time.
