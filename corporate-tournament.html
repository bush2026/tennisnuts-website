About the Tournament

Where office colleagues become tennis teammates.

The Tennisnuts Corporate Tournament is Pune's first corporate tennis team championship — a format where companies form teams, players represent their organisations on court, and the competition is as real as it gets. It's not just about who wins; it's about what happens when working professionals who love tennis get to play together, against each other, in the spirit the game demands.

The inaugural edition in March 2023 saw eight companies from Pune's IT and corporate sector field teams, some of them finding teammates through the Tennisnuts platform itself. The entire draw was played without a single line call dispute — mature, humble players who came to compete and made new friends in the process.

Beyond the competition, the Corporate Tournament is a networking platform: new friendships across company lines, contacts made at the net, and a corporate tennis community in Pune that is only going to grow.
