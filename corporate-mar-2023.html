The Story

Tournament Recap

An overwhelming response from Pune's corporate sector set the tone from the very start. Eight companies formed teams — their employees making weekends available from busy working schedules — and showed up ready to compete. What followed was the city's first Corporate Tennis Team Championship: company vs company, colleagues representing their organisations on the clay courts of AWTA NCL.

"It was a true example of team effort — there was no instance of line call disputes. Lovely people, matured and humble players. Tennis played in the very best spirit."

Organised by Tennisnuts under the aegis of PMDTA, the tournament drew players who had found each other through the platform itself — some companies could form their teams only after enquiring on Tennisnuts, which made the whole event feel like a genuine community achievement. The championship was played in extraordinary spirit: competitive, intense, and entirely free of dispute.

Cognizant were the standout team throughout — consistent in every match, composed under pressure, and deserving champions. Their victory was met with warm and genuine appreciation from the whole field. Probity Software ran them close and played wonderful tennis of their own across the draw.

The presentation ceremony was graced by Sunder Iyer Sir, MSLTA Secretary and AITA Honorary Secretary, and Himanshu Gosavi Sir, Hon. Joint Secretary PMDTA — both of whom shared their vision for corporate and grassroots tennis in Pune. Their presence underlined just how significant this inaugural event was for the city's tennis calendar.

The event also ran in parallel with the PMDTA Jr. Masters — a testament to the sheer organisational effort involved. Beyond tennis itself, the tournament lived up to its promise as a networking platform: new friendships were made, contacts exchanged, and the unique community of corporate tennis players in Pune grew a little stronger.

Heartfelt thanks to Venky for all ground-0 efforts across the Seniors and Corporate events. To Amit Kindo and Bhushan Sardesai for being present and supportive throughout. To Resham and Sardar Singh for being flexible enough to manage both the PMDTA Jr. Masters and the Corporate Championship simultaneously. To Pravin Sir and his staff, and to Kiran Dada for his as-always reliable support. A special thank you to Abhishek Tamhane Sir, Hon. Secretary PMDTA and MSLTA Councillor, for the encouragement and belief to launch this format. And to Solinco for supporting the tournament.
