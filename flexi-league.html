About Flexi League

A league you fit around your life.

The Tennisnuts Flexi League is competitive doubles reimagined for real life. Instead of one packed weekend, the league runs over several weeks — giving you the competitive edge of a proper league format with the flexibility to play when both teams are ready.

Register as a pair, get placed into a balanced group, and coordinate directly with the opposing team to pick a time and venue that works. Split the court costs, play your match, and update the standings. No organisers, no fixed slots — just two teams who want to compete.

Once every team in your group has played, the top pairs advance to a Knockout Weekend hosted at CPR — quarterfinals, semifinals, and a final all in one day. Trophies, T-shirts, and full media coverage for every participant.

How It Works

Three steps to the trophy.

A proper competitive structure — without the constraint of showing up on one specific day for your league matches.

01
📋

Register Your Pair

Sign up with your doubles partner. You'll be placed into a balanced group based on experience. Entry fee is per team and covers the full league including the Knockout Weekend.

02
📅

Schedule Your Matches

Contact the other teams in your group and agree on a time, date, and venue that suits both sides. Court booking costs and balls are shared equally. Play anytime during the league window.

🏆

Knockout Weekend

Top 2 teams from each group advance. Quarterfinals, semifinals, and the final are all held on a single designated weekend at CPR — no court fees for knockout matches.

Match Rules

Proper tennis. Properly scored.

No short sets or super tiebreakers to rush things — league matches are full tennis. Here's the scoring detail.

League Stage

  • Round Robin format within groups
  • Best of 3 sets per match
  • Full sets played — no short-set format
  • Deuce: Best-of-3 point format (first to win 2 points)
  • At 6–6: standard 7-point tie-break (win by 2)
  • Third set: Super Tie-Break, first to 10 points (win by 2)
  • Top 2 teams from each group qualify for knockouts

Scheduling & Logistics

  • Teams mutually agree on venue and match time
  • Court booking charges shared equally
  • Balls: Wilson Tour or Sky Warrior recommended
  • CPR courts available throughout the league at 15% off during off-peak hours
  • All knockout matches held at CPR, no court fees
  • Top 4 league teams are seeded for knockout draw
