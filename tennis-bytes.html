About the Show

Tennis Talk, Pune Style

Tennis Bytes is Tennisnuts' podcast where we talk about everything tennis. From match analysis to player interviews, from the Sopal Trophy to Wimbledon — no topic is off limits. We bring you the voices of Pune's tennis community alongside commentary on the biggest moments in the global game.

New episodes drop every two weeks. Free to listen, always honest, and packed with tennis conversations you won't find anywhere else.
