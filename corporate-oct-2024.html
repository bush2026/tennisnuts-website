The Story

Tournament Recap

If the first edition proved there was appetite for corporate tennis in Pune, the second edition proved it was here to stay. Twelve companies — up from eight in 2023 — formed teams, split into four groups, and turned up across two days for what became one of the most competitive weekends on the Tennisnuts calendar.

The format stepped up to match the ambition. A full Round Robin group stage on Saturday — two sessions, 9AM to 1PM and then 5:30PM to 9PM — fed into quarter-finals and knockouts on Sunday. Tour Director Venky got the draws done, and Atul took charge of the detailed scheduling. The machine ran smoothly, save for one force of nature: a rain interruption on Day 1 that pushed some group stage matches to Sunday morning before the quarter-finals could begin.

"Repeat of last year again — CTS I vs Probity Software in the final. The same two teams, the same intensity, and a super tie-break to decide it all."

When the dust settled on the knockout rounds, the final produced exactly the rematch everyone wanted: CTS I vs Probity Software. Same final as 2023. Same stage, same pressure — but a very different result. Last year, Cognizant took it. This time, Probity Software held their nerve through a nail-biting match that went to a super tie-break, claiming the title and earning themselves what Alok, watching on, perfectly described as "sweet revenge."

The quality of tennis throughout the weekend was exceptional — four groups, twelve teams, all competing with the kind of intensity and good spirit that has become the hallmark of this tournament. Not a single complaint from any quarter. Just competitors who love the game, playing hard and making friends along the way.

Thanks to Venky for Tour Director duties and getting the draws right. To Atul for taking on the complex scheduling task. To Sudhir for organising and keeping the whole event on track across two days. To Amit for his support throughout. And to all the court maintenance staff for keeping the surface in great shape despite the rain interruption.
