What We Do

Coached by Pros, Loved by Everyone

Tennisnuts runs regular group clinics with experienced, certified coaches. Every session is designed to be engaging, practical, and fun — covering footwork, groundstrokes, volleys, and match tactics. Whether you've never held a racket or you've been playing for years, there's a clinic for you.

We keep batches small — a maximum of 8 players per session — so you get real attention, real feedback, and real improvement. Clinics run on weekends at courts across Pune.

  • ✓ Saturdays & Sundays
  • ✓ Multiple Pune venues
  • ✓ Rackets available on request
Skill Levels

Find Your Batch

Beginner
Starting Fresh

For those new to tennis or returning after a break. We cover the fundamentals: grip, stance, footwork, and basic groundstrokes. The goal is to build confidence and fall in love with the sport — no pressure, just great tennis.

Intermediate
Level Up Your Game

For club players who want to sharpen consistency, improve tactical awareness, and become more competitive in match play. We work on shot selection, net play, second serve, and reading your opponent.

Advanced
Tournament Ready

For competitive players focused on technique refinement and tournament preparation. High-intensity sessions covering advanced patterns of play, mental toughness, and match-specific conditioning.
