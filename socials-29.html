The Story

Edition Recap

Long wait. Finally compromised — 16 entries, limited deliberately, amid COVID and social distancing. The Socials were back. And the relief was palpable on every court.

Dr Ravi Kothari and Nitin Sawant — Teja — won the title. Accurate, powerful serves from Nitin. Magical movement and net game from Dr Ravi. A combination that proved impossible to beat on the day, even in a final against Venky and Sudhir — who had come so close. The Tennisnuts Rolling Trophy got its second entry: No.28 Venky & Kannan, No.29 Dr Ravi & Nitin.

"The rolling trophy. No.28 — Venky & Kannan. No.29 — Dr Ravi & Nitin. The names are going on."

The community showed up in the best possible way. Gajanan Kulkarni Sir — senior-most on court — went deep before going out in the semis. Joy came twice to the venue despite being injured, anchoring the trophy distribution ceremony with Kumar. Ankit's work encouraging tennis at PCMC was visible. Amit Kindo organized every small detail across planning and scheduling. The Mont Vert crew were there. Manish's big serves, his magical touches around the legs, his overhead smashes — bliss to watch.

Thanks to Amit Kindo for every small nitty-gritty — planning, safety, scheduling, groups, knockouts. All brilliantly done. To Joy for coming despite injury to anchor the ceremony. To everyone who came back after the long wait. To those who couldn't make it this time — next Socials, no one gets left out.
