About the Championship

Where Clubs Come Together to Compete

The Manegrow Narendra Sopal Club Championship is an annual inter-club tennis tournament open to club teams from Pune and PCMC. Teams compete across multiple age categories in a doubles format — with each tie decided by cumulative games across all matches.

Organised by Tennisnuts under the aegis of the Pune District Tennis Association (PMDTA), the championship has grown rapidly since its inaugural edition in 2022 — bringing together players from across the city who compete fiercely but play in the true spirit of the game.

In Memoriam

Narendra Sopal

The Manegrow Narendra Sopal Club Championship is held in memory of the late Narendra Sopal — a beloved figure in Pune's tennis community. The championship honours his legacy by bringing the city's clubs together each year in the sport he loved. Presented by title sponsor Manegrow in partnership with Tennisnuts and Tower Transmission.
