About the Tournament

Pune's Most Beloved Amateur Tennis Event

The Sopal Trophy is an annual singles and doubles tournament open to all skill levels. It celebrates community, competition, and camaraderie on the court. Whether you're picking up a racket for the first time or a seasoned club player, the Sopal Trophy has a draw for you.

Founded in 2022, the tournament has grown every year — attracting players from across Pune who come to compete, make friends, and enjoy the sport they love. It's more than a tournament. It's a tradition.
