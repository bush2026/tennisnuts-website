About Tennisnuts Open

The most competitive tennis Tennisnuts has to offer.

Tennisnuts Open is our flagship tournament format — open to players of all ages, all levels of competitive experience, and featuring both Singles and Doubles draws. Juniors who've been competing for years, seniors who refuse to slow down, and everyone in between share the same courts, the same pressure, and the same prize: a Tennisnuts podium.

Unlike our category-specific events, the Open is a battlefield with no divisions by age. That means a 45-year-old doubles specialist might face a 22-year-old on the rise — and the experienced pair might just come out on top. It makes for unpredictable, emotionally charged tennis.

Held 2–3 times a year, complex scheduling is handled with care so players get back-to-back matches and the whole event stays tight and exciting. Come ready to compete.
