About Seniors Tennis

Competitive tennis for those who know the game best.

The PMDTA Tennisnuts Tour - Seniors is a competitive doubles series for players aged 35 and above. Held 2–3 times a year, each tournament features group-stage play followed by knockout rounds — fast-paced, high-quality tennis with the camaraderie that only a shared love of the game can build.

Conducted under the aegis of the Pune Metropolitan District Tennis Association (PMDTA) and powered by Sportsjam, these tournaments bring together Pune's finest senior doubles pairs in a competitive but welcoming environment.

Whether it's your first competitive doubles outing or you've been playing seniors tennis for years, there's a place for you here. Rain delays, short-notice formats, and all — the show always goes on.
