The Story

Edition Recap

"Happy that this is becoming a platform for every tennis enthusiast — to experience good competition, to play with different age groups, to face those who have been professionals."

Socials #35 was a true cross-section of Pune tennis. Brigadier Ajit Sir, 65+, stepped on court to inspire youngsters — and inspired everyone watching too. Daksh Patil, a U12 national player, showed his talent against seniors twice his age and went home with new friends. The range of players, backgrounds, and generations on court made this edition something special.

The tennis itself delivered too. Sachin and Girish played fabulous tennis as expected. Aditya and Manish were equally impressive. The quarter-final comeback by Daksh and Amit K against Sunil and Nitin was one of the gutsiest moments of any Social — a true two-player partnership refusing to go away. From Undri, Houzaifa and friends made the trip across Pune and Girija was the one girl on court — great game from her.

Off the court, this edition ran a used shoes and clothes drive for needy tennis coaches and markers — supported generously by the tennis fraternity. And someone brought homemade idli chutney. Mandatory attendance, brilliant feedback.

Sportsjam vouchers of Rs.250, Rs.500 and Rs.750 for podium finishers, along with medals and trophies. Thanks to Kiran Dada, Pravin Sir, team and security guards at the facility for looking after us so well.
